In an increasingly digital world, the role of architecture is becoming increasingly important. From designing structures and buildings to mapping out cities and towns, architects are playing a growing role in shaping our world. But why? The importance of architecture goes beyond aesthetics; it affects how we live, learn and collaborate with others.
